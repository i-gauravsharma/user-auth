EaseCare - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to EaseCare, a water tracking application designed to help you monitor and improve your hydration habits. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data when you use the EaseCare application.

By using EaseCare, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the application.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

When you register for an EaseCare account, we may collect personal information, including your name, email address, age, gender, and other optional profile information.

b. Usage Data:

We may collect data about your use of the application, such as the amount of water you consume, the time and frequency of use, and your interactions with the app's features.

c. Device Information:

We may collect information about the device you use to access EaseCare, including device type, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

a. Provide and Personalize Services:

To provide you with personalized water intake recommendations, reminders, and tracking features.

To improve the EaseCare app based on user feedback and usage patterns.

b. Communication:

To send you updates, notifications, and information related to your use of the app.

c. Analytics:

To analyze usage data and trends, helping us understand user behavior and preferences to enhance our services.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

a. Service Providers:

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the app, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer support services.

b. Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information when required by law, such as in response to a legal process, court order, or government request.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your data:

a. Access and Update:

You can access and update your personal information through the app's settings.

b. Communication Preferences:

You can opt out of receiving non-essential communications from us.

7. Children's Privacy

EaseCare is not intended for children under the age of 10. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children under 10 years of age.
